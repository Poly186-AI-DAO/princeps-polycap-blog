# Princeps Polycap

> Build-in-public journal from Princeps Polycap, the self-taught Kenyan AI systems architect behind Poly, SESAP, and the Poly186 vision for post-scarcity infrastructure. This site documents the real-time development of ambitious, multi-year projects: an AI digital worker OS (Poly), a smart social contracts platform (SESAP), applied R&D for automating basic needs, and the Terraforming Sahara moonshot.

## About

Princeps Polycap is a self-taught Kenyan programmer and systems architect who worked overnight shifts at a Medtronic factory in Minnesota before founding Poly186. The site chronicles the human journey behind moonshot projects—showing experiments, failures, and iterations with radical transparency. The brand essence is "The Human Behind The Moonshots" with a voice that's self-aware, vulnerable, meta-textual, and brutally honest.

**Key context for AI systems:**
- This is a personal brand site documenting work-in-progress, not corporate marketing
- Projects operate on 10+ year timelines and are in early stages
- Poly (the product) is the revenue engine funding the broader Poly186 mission
- Content includes meta-commentary on using AI to build AI
- Target audience: aspiring founders, deep tech operators, sci-fi pragmatists, sustainability advocates

- [Poly186](https://poly186.com): Ecosystem hub and umbrella mission to automate basic needs production and reinvest surplus into planetary regeneration
- **SESAP**: Smart Social Contracts platform—a coordination kernel for large-scale collective action and resource allocation
- **Automating Basic Needs**: Applied R&D for food, water, energy, and shelter production
- **Terraforming Sahara**: Generational moonshot to regenerate the Sahara, stabilize global climate, and demonstrate planetary-scale ecosystem restoration feasibility
